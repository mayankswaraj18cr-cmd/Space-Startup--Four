📅 Space history — this date

December 21, 1971 — the Soviet Meteor satellite carried the first operational Hall-effect thruster (SPT-50) to orbit, beginning five decades of electric-propulsion flight heritage that today powers Starlink, GSAT, and most modern GEO comsats.
